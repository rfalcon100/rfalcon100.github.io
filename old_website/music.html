Yuniper - Divide and Conquer

Yuniper is the new musical project from Hector Zarate and Ricardo Falcon. Despite both being computer engineering majors, their musical backgrounds could hardly be more different. Hector has a long career in IDM, while Ricardo is a hard rock guitar virtuoso. The resulting music is forward looking electronics with a pop twist. Their debut EP, "Divide and Conquer" masterfully samples, slices, mixes and sequences analogical sources (guitar riffs, field recordings, radio signals, magnetic tape and pedals) transformed through guitar amplifiers and other analogical devices. Melodic IDM with a touch of prog rock, 8-bit synthesizers, tons of guitars and other evocative sounds.


Recorded, composed, played and produced by Hector Zarate Rea and Ricardo Falcon Perez
Master: DJ Manducator
Executive production for Discos Konfort: Axel Barcelo
