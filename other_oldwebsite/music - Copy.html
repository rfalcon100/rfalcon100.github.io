Yuniper - Divide and Conquer

Yuniper is the new musical project from Hector Zarate and Ricardo Falcon. Despite both being computer engineering majors, their musical backgrounds could hardly be more different. Hèctor has a long career in IDM, while Ricardo is a hard rock guitar virtuoso. The resulting music is forward looking electronics with a pop twist. Their debut EP, "Divide and Conquer" masterfully samples, slices, mixes and sequences analogical sources (guitar riffs, field recordings, radio signals, magnetic tape and pedals) transformed through guitar amplifiers and other analogical devices. Melodic IDM with a touch of prog rock, 8-bit synthesizers, tons of guitars and other evocative sounds.

Recorded, composed, played and produced by Héctor Zárate Rea and Ricardo Falcón Pérez

Executive production for Discos Konfort: Axel Barceló

Star Goa - Prototype

For my Bachelor thesis I built an application to study audio and genetic algorithms. The idea behind the project was to use additive synthesis to replicate short sounds, using a combination of sine waves (and other waveforms). The concept was inspired by Roger Johansson's project that replicates images using a combination of simple polygons obtained with genetic algorithms.

Key Features:

  • Evolution parameters highly customizable.
  • Presents comprehensive information about the evolution process.
  • Applies a standard ADSR envelope.
  • Available waveforms include sine, triangular, square and sawtooth.
